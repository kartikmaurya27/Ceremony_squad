Quick Answers

Before You Reach Out

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
We guarantee a response within 4 business hours on weekdays. For urgent event-day support, we have a 24/7 emergency line dedicated to active event clients.
Can I visit the studio for a consultation?
Absolutely! Our Noida studio at Sector 63 is open Monday–Saturday. Please call or WhatsApp ahead to schedule a dedicated 60-minute consultation with a senior event director.
Do you offer video consultations?
Yes. We conduct Zoom and Google Meet consultations for clients outside NCR, NRIs planning India events, and anyone who prefers remote meetings. Just mention it in your enquiry.
What details should I include in my first message?
The more context the better — event type, approximate date, location, guest count and any specific ideas or themes you have in mind. Even a rough brief helps us tailor our first response precisely to your needs.
What's the fastest way to reach you?
WhatsApp is the fastest — +91 98765 43210. For detailed enquiries, the contact form above is ideal. For urgent calls, dial directly during studio hours (9 AM – 8 PM, Mon–Sat).
Are there any consultation charges?
Initial consultations — whether in-person, by phone or video — are completely free of charge. We believe in transparency from day one and never charge for discovery conversations.
